# HUL Investment Summary

## Company

Hindustan Unilever Limited (HUL) is one of India's largest FMCG companies with a strong portfolio of consumer brands across food, personal care, and home care segments.

---

## Business Strengths

- Strong brand portfolio
- Market leadership in several FMCG categories
- Wide distribution network
- Stable cash flow generation

---

## Key Drivers Considered in the Model

The valuation model focuses on the following drivers:

1. Revenue growth trends
2. Operating margins
3. Capital expenditure
4. Working capital requirements
5. Long-term growth expectations

---

## Valuation Approach

The company is valued using a Discounted Cash Flow approach.

Steps followed:

1. Forecast revenue and operating profit
2. Estimate Free Cash Flow
3. Discount cash flows using WACC
4. Calculate terminal value
5. Estimate enterprise value
